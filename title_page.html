Editor's Notes

This html version of The St. Petersburg Diaries (1843–1849) of Anna McNeill Whistler differs from the published pdf version only in that page numbers are not supported and that minor typographical errors discovered during its production have been rectified. The typographical errors will thus not appear in the second edition of The St. Petersburg Diaries (1843–1849) of Anna McNeill Whistler. For citation purposes, please refer to the pdf version.

The website was designed by Karyn Huenemann, editorial assistant to Dr. Evelyn Harden. All comments, questions, or corrections can be directed to anna.whistler.diary@gmail.com.
